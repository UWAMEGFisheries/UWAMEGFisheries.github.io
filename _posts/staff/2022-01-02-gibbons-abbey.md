## Role
<img src='/images/abbey-gibbons-profile.png' align='right' width="350" hspace="20" vspace="10">
My main roles in the UWA Marine Ecology Fisheries research lab include video analysis, species identification, image analysis, data wrangling and synthesising for marine park reporting and helping prepare, calibrate and deploy BOSS and BRUV systems for fieldwork.

## About me
I grew up in Perth and completed my Bachelor of Science in Wildlife Conservation and Biology, at Murdoch University in 2023. After completing my degree I spent my time aiding wildlife rehabilitation, mainly birds but occasionally mammals and reptiles. I am now working at the UWA Marine Ecology Group Fisheries research lab as a research assistant for Dr. Tim Langlois.
