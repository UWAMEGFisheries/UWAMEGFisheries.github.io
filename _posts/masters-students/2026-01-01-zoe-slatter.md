## About Me
I grew up in Walpole and Albany and I completed my Bachelor’s in Marine Science in 2025, before starting a Masters in Environmental Science, specialising in Marine and Coastal Management. I have had a deep love for the sea since I was very young and I have always wanted to study the ocean, so this is a dream come true. I have volunteered on a range of projects with CSIRO, particularly invertebrates but fish have a special place in my heart. I have the absolute privilege of having Dr Tim Langlois and Dr Cindy Bessey supervising my project investigating whether MaxN by size class for key demersal species can give better size structure and abundance within Geographe Bay Marine Park.
